A comprehensive suite of wind tunnel experimental campaigns conducted for the **Experimental Fluid Dynamics** course at **Politecnico di Milano (A.A. 2024–2025)**.  

This repository contains the **data analysis, post-processing scripts, and technical reports** for three distinct aerodynamic measurement techniques used to characterize aerodynamic flows.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this repository is to characterize aerodynamic phenomena using **industry-standard experimental techniques**.  

The laboratory campaigns progress from **basic aerodynamic force measurements** to **advanced optical flow field diagnostics**.

---

# 🧪 Lab 1: Force Measurements

Calibration of an **external force balance** and aerodynamic characterization of an airfoil.

**Technique**

- External force balance

**Key Results**

- Computation of aerodynamic coefficients:
  - Lift coefficient \(C_L\)
  - Drag coefficient \(C_D\)
  - Pitching moment coefficient \(C_M\)

- Measurements performed across different **angles of attack**

- Generation of the **airfoil aerodynamic polar**

---

# 🧪 Lab 2: Pressure & Velocity Measurements

Detailed aerodynamic characterization of a **NACA 23012 airfoil**.

**Techniques**

- Surface pressure transducers
- Hot-wire anemometry

**Key Results**

- Integration of **surface pressure distributions**
- Measurement of the **wake velocity deficit**
- Estimation of total drag including:
  - viscous contributions
  - pressure drag
- Identification of **stall conditions**

---

# 🧪 Lab 3: Particle Image Velocimetry (PIV)

Quantitative flow field characterization around a pair of **co-rotating propellers**.

**Technique**

- Particle Image Velocimetry (PIV)

**Key Results**

- Time-resolved and spatially resolved **velocity field measurements**
- Comparison between:
  - **custom PIV post-processing algorithms**
  - **robust open-source solutions** such as PIVlab
- Extraction of:
  - velocity fields
  - vorticity distributions
